FAQ

What does this app do?

Paparazzi uses face detection to blur out the faces in the photographs that you take.

How do I know what’s going to get blurred out?

The camera preview shows the blurring in real time, so you can see exactly what will be blurred and what won’t.

Will it detect every face?

Not really. It should find most of the faces most of the time, but it’s not perfect. You should always check the preview to see what’s being blurred.

Are there different styles to the blurring?

There are two; you can choose between a pixelated effect and a black square.
